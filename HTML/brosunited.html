Bro's United Mentorship Program

Where bros can be bros.



About Bro's United

Bro's United is a big brother-little brother mentorship program that allows children on any end of the autism spectrum to have a place where they can truly have fun but most importantly be themselves. Us here at Theta Delta Chi at the University of Washington truly value the relationships between our little bros and strive to ensure comfort in the lives of our little brothers. We know that every child with autism is different, but at the very end of the day they are normal people just like us.
