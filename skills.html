Voices for Autism's Pre-made Alexa Skills

Our little Bro's value the Bro's United Mentorship program because it provides them a fun and safe environment where they can truly be themselves. Bro's United allows students with autism to come together as a community with a higher education group and practice on their social skills in a judge-free zone. Voices for Autism is built on the foundation of Bro's United by allowing students to practice their retention and cognitive skills, all at the comfort of their own home with the use of an Amazon Echo Dot by using the custom Alexa skills tailored to the Bro's United program.

Big Brother Jeopardy

Big Brother Jeopardy is a Bro's United trivia game that teaches students about daily tasks and fun facts about Bro's United.
